3. Problem Index by Constraint

By Difficulty Level

  • Beginner: Basic checking problems (1-4, Subarray 1-4)
  • Intermediate: Counting and length-based problems (5-18, Subarray 5-19)
  • Advanced: Optimization and pattern-based problems (19+, Subarray 20+)

By Common Patterns

  • Dynamic Programming: LCS, LIS, Maximum sum problems
  • Sliding Window: Longest/Shortest subarray problems
  • Two Pointers: Subsequence checking, subarray sum problems
  • Prefix Sums: Subarray sum problems, divisibility checks
  • Hash Maps: Count problems, distinct element problems
